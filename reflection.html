Personal Reflection

My journey in computer science has been shaped by academic challenges and hands on experience that strengthened my foundation. These challenges and experiences pushed me to continue getting a degree and building mental fortitude as well as a greater understanding of technology.

Balancing professional software engineering work with my degree allowed me to confidently apply theory to practice. This also allowed me to experiment with time managment skills and networking while still in the earliest stages of my career.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu has taught me discipline, resilience, and problem solving under pressure, skills that directly influence how I approach complex software challenges. These practical skills are priceless and motivate me to continue honing my craft professionally as well as my hobbies.

My long term goal is to grow as an engineer while contributing to impactful projects that solve real problems. I want to enjoy the work that I do and leverage the skills I've made so far to help people. With the shifting times, software might not be a focus for a lot of careers so I intend to take these skills into whatever job I decide to persue.

Seth Keenan — February 15, 2026
